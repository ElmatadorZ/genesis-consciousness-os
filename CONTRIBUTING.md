# Contributing to Genesis Consciousness OS

## Ways to Contribute

### Share Your Build
Built your own Consciousness OS using this framework?  
Open a Discussion → "Show Your Build" category.

Include:
- Your name/handle
- Your primary domain
- What you changed or extended
- What surprised you about the architecture

### Report a Cognitive Edge Case
Found a situation where the system produces unexpected output?  
Open an Issue with:
- Input that triggered it
- What you expected
- What happened instead
- Your hypothesis about why

### Suggest Skill Additions
Have a domain skill that would benefit others?  
Open a Discussion → "Skill Proposals".

### Documentation
See something unclear in the docs?  
PRs welcome for documentation improvements.

---

## What We're Building Toward

A library of cognitive architectures — one per person.

The vision: anyone can build their own Consciousness OS,  
calibrated to how they actually think,  
and share it with others who think similarly.

The ElmatadorZ version is the reference implementation.  
Your version is the contribution.
